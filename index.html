TOPIC: MOVIE PRODUCTION



Movie production has been around for over one hundred years and has been the foundation of entertainment for the world. There are many different fields that cover movie production like the editing portion, film producing, and the directors directing everyone behind the scenes. Technology is the creation of how we come to know of movies and shows today, and can still continue to progress them in a number of ways. PART A



Beginner Level

pCam Pro
It has 26 essential cinematography & photography tools. Only works on Apple iOS devices. It is simple and anyone can understand it. You can use this on your phone to edit or record on the go. Frequently updated and currently with over 1600 cameras. Additional features are always in development.

Capcut
Video editing software that is available both on Android and iOS. CapCut offers basic elements like text effects, music, transitions, and filters all everything else is coded in. Even without any experience editing a video, you can have your content produced in minutes. Even though all the features are free of cost, unlocking advanced effects, additional fonts, and cloud space is available by purchasing CapCut Pro. Perfect for no experience.

Shot Designer
It is known as one of the best apps for filmaking because it covers a lot of grounds. It can automatically make certain camera movements. Create detailed shot lists. It also includes tutorials and is very easy to use due to excellent explanations. Although, it is very expensive to use.

All of these software that are apps are very accessible and easy to use to the user. They are all filled with tutorials and do most of the work for you. They have a wide range of effects and different characteristics to each one. They import to any device and you can work on your projects on the go!

Intermediate Level

3D Printing
Allows for quick and accurate creation of custom props and costumes. Can print complex designs that are hard or expensive to make traditionally. Saves time and reduces costs by speeding up production. Offers creative possibilities with different materials. Can be expensive, depending on the complexity and materials used.

WeVideo
Has tutorials that can help you in case you are stuck. Limitations in effects/transitions. Can handle audio and not just video formats. Library of royalty-free music and sound effects and animation transitions.

These software and hardware provide prior knowledge to using it. For WeVideo, it is possible to work on it as a beginner but it is suggested that you need knowledge about the basics of video editing prior to working with the app. There are no tutorials implemented in the software but there is some explanation on how to begin using the app on Youtube if you donâ€™t already know. And 3D printing seems like something easy to do but it is hard. You must create and apply the design that you want to 3D print onto the printer and make sure the hardware is working properly so the design can be made. It is also important to have the right materials to print the design. Additionally, it is important to be aware of the limitations and capabilities of the printer. Finally, it is important to be patient and aware of any issues that may arise.

Professional Level

DJI Inspire 2
Provides a stable aerial view with no camera shake, perfect for movie production. Great for capturing landscapes or actors performing from above. Features a built-in camera with customizable settings like frames and filters. Ideal for high-quality aerial shots and dynamic filming angles.

Moon Smart Focus
A smart focus ring with precise, automatic focus tracking. Zooms in on the tiniest details, improving production quality. Removes the need for manual focus adjustments, making filming more efficient. Features a sharp lens and is ideal for dynamic scenes. Controlled via an app for easy adjustments on the go.

After Effects
After Effects is used for motion graphics and visual effects. It includes compositing and animation tools. Works well with other Adobe products like Premiere Pro. Extendable using plugins and scripts. Used extensively in film and digital media for editing.

These are the advanced things I found on the internet while searching for professional movie production software and hardware. The DJI Inspire 2 drone and the Moon Smart Focus are both things used out in the field when filming. They are used to get complex shots of the scene and make things cinematic. And After Effects is where those shots are imported and improved to fit perfectly and display itself like a movie. After Effects has a lot of settings and different ways to use it and would be very complicated for someone who is not trained in video editing. It would take 3 hours alone just to show someone all the controls that this limitless software uses.

My tool: Aframe

A-Frame can be employed to create a virtual replica of a movie set or stage so that directors and crew can visualize how things will appear prior to shooting. They may visit the set virtually, play around with camera angles, and see how lighting affects the set, all through a web browser. This makes planning scenes easier and reduces mistakes when actually producing them. It's a valuable previsualization aid, both time and budget-consuming. Like seen when you click, it shows a small display of a black ball and a directional light being shined on it. This light is coming from stage right, which is the left of the audience. This tool can also show different models of how advanced hardware or new inventions can look like. Aframe is really resourceful, and a lot of their information on how to use it or examples are already on the website.
